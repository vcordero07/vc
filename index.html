projects

*rikuchikuy-list

Rikuchikuy-list is an app that allows you to store and share your wish list. I created this app to provide an alternative solution to sharing links with your families for the holidays.

*tekkojuku-cal

Tekkojuku-cal is an app that allows the head instructor to automate the process of the monthly calendar class assignment. You can view, add, edit, and delete classes by going to the Calendar page. I created this app to help the dojo that I attend automate the process for their monthly calendar.

*bus-forecast

Bus-forecast is an app that allows you to get the MBTA bus predictions, as well as the weather for that location. You have two modes, search by stop or by location. I created this app because I commute daily and I have to check the weather often before leaving home.
